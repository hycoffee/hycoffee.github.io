Hybrid Coffe Project

Our coffee is special. And we're not just saying that! By specialising on organic, fairly traded hybrid coffee varieties we are offering you a cup that tastes great and does it's share towards climate change mitigation. Our value chains are hyper-localised, meaning the coffee is grown, picked and roasted locally (all the stuff that brings in money) and directly benefits small-holder producers in Timor-Leste.
